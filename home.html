Welcome

Ahsanullah University of Science & Technology (AUST), Sponsored by the Dhaka Ahsania Mission and approved by the Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh, Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology has been successfully carrying out its noble mission since 1995. It maintains close collaboration with the University Grants Commission (UGC), Bangladesh and many other national and international educational institutions and professional bodies.
